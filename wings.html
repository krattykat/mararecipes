Japanese Teriyaki Chicken Wings Recipe

Posted by KrattyKat

This is a quick example HTML project rebuilding the recipe originally created by the website NoRecipes. This website was built using HTML5.

Ingredients

  • Chicken wings
  • Soy sauce
  • Sugar
  • Sake
  • Aromatics
  • Garnish

Preparation

  1. Add ginger and garlic into a pot and then place the wings in it.
  2. Pour the sugar, soy sauce, and sake onto the chicken, and then put the pot over high heat to bring the liquid to a boil.
  3. Cover the pot with a lid and turn down the heat to maintain a steady simmer. Let this braise for 15 minutes for the sauce to soak into the chicken and for the fat in the chicken to render out.
  4. While the chicken is braising, preheat the oven to 430 degrees F (220 C) and prepare a sheet pan by placing a metal cooling rack on top.
  5. When the timer is up, transfer the wings to the rack and let the surface of the chicken dry out for a few minutes.
  6. Put the pan in the oven and bake them until the skin is a rich brown color.
  7. To finish the teriyaki glaze, strain the braising liquid into another pot and skim off the excess fat using a spoon. Boil the braising liquid to reduce it until the bubbles are big and shiny and itâ€™s relatively thick.
  8. Once your chicken teriyaki wings are down browning, remove them from the oven and brush or spoon on the teriyaki sauce.
  9. You're done, now eat! :)
