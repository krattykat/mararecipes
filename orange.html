Orange Chicken Recipe (Panda Express "Copy-Cat" Recipe)

Posted by KrattyKat
This is a quick example HTML project rebuilding the recipe originally created by the website Cooking With Mima. This website was built using HTML5.

Ingredients

  • Orange juice
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Fresh ground black pepper
  • Corn syrup
  • Brown sugar or honey
  • Fresh garlic
  • Grated ginger
  • Vegetable or sesame oil
  • Cayanne pepper or chili flakes (Only if you want to make it spicy!)

Preparation

  1. To prepare the chicken, cut the boneless chicken thighs or tenders into small pieces, about 1/2 inch long.
  2. Toss the chicken pieces in a bowl and coat with the flour, corn starch, black pepper, onion powder, salt and ginger powder.
  3. In a deep fraying pan, fry the pieces of chicken leaving some space between them to avoid over crowding. They will only need to be fried for about 2-3 minutes at the most until golden brown, because the pieces are small. Over cooking the chicken makes them chewy so be careful there.
  4. Once the chicken is done cooking, drain well using a spider strainer spoon and transfer over to the cooked sauce. Toss well with the sauce and allow the chicken and the sauce to cook together for another 1-2 minutes.
  5. Serve this simple Orange Chicken Recipe with some homemade Lo Mein Noodles or over white rice. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions to add color (optional).
